# Evening Rain Design System

## The Evening Rain Theme (Foundational)

The Evening Rain theme is the core color palette for Evening Rain Website, provides an idea environment for reading comfortably in a dark mode.

### Design Principles
Use these design principles for overall designs.

1. **Immersive**: The design immerses the reader and holds them into reading.
2. **Subtle**: The design is sophisticated and subtle. It is so good that it's invisible to the reader.
3. **Minimalist**: Clean, distraction-free design
4. **Reading Friendly**: Readers can read the texts easily, can skim and scan, easily understand blocks. The design is not cluttered. 
5. **Dark Themed**: Dark Color palette is used in a way that is comfortable to read for long period of time.
6. **Consistent**: The design is consisten across different parts and components.
7. **Responsive**: The design smoothly fits diffferent device and screen sizes.
8. **Quality**: Modern, Elegant, Minimal, Tasteful.
